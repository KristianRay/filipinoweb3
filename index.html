Filipino Web3 Community Builders

is an organization with the aim of helping our fellow Filipinos hone their skills and maximize their potential to grow in the Web3 space.

Get Started

TO WEB3 AND BEYOND!

We are helping to build a healthy group of Web3 enthusiasts and community builders, and we want YOU to be part of it.

Learn More
Vision

To be the premium hub for Web3 community builders that is focused on bringing holistic, connected, and innovative principles in Web3 community building.

Mission

Provide valuable information and resources for the community that will help with upscaling skills in Web3 community building and development.

Provide a channel for community builders to exchange ideas, information, and opportunities.

To help Professionalize the field of Web3 community building through continuing education and training.

Our Values

Holistic

Community building in Web3 involves different roles that are essential in its development. Maintaining the balance and flow of interdependence in the community is essential for community development. As builders, we must develop and strengthen the shared goals and ideals of our communities.

Connected

Web3 development should be forged with sincere and real connections. Strategies must be attuned to the communityâ€™s needs and interests. The advocacy of the organization is a Community Centric approach to Community Development.

Innovative

Web3 is characterized by its fast-paced dynamics. It is important for Web3 Community builders to be highly adaptable. The organization aims to foster a vibrant exchange of information and encourage continuing education.
